Turistic places that everyone must visit!
Machu Picchu
Description: Ancient Incan city set in the Andes, rediscovered in 1911. Known for its stunning ruins and panoramic views.
Location: Southeast Peru, in the Andes Mountains, around 2,430 meters above sea level.
Eiffel Tower
Description: Iconic Parisian landmark built in 1889, designed by Gustave Eiffel. A symbol of Paris and one of the most visited monuments.
Location: Champ de Mars, near the Seine River in Paris, 324 meters tall.
Great Wall of China
Description: Ancient fortification built to protect China, stretching over thousands of kilometers. A major historical and architectural marvel.
Location: Extends across northern China, with a notable section near Beijing.
Taj Mahal
Description: Stunning white marble mausoleum built in the 17th century by Emperor Shah Jahan for his wife. A UNESCO World Heritage site.
Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India, beside the Yamuna River.
